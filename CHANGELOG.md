  - `\logmean`
  - `\flow`
  - `\difference`
  - `\nablaoperator`
  - `\vect`
  - `\deriv`
  - `\fracderiv`
  - `\timederiv`
  - `\posderiv`
- New tabular showcasing those built-in math macros (119fce11)
- New tabular showcasing the built-in glossary commands (0e7ed5a9):
  - `\idx`
  - `\name`
  - `\sym`
  - `\sub`
  - `\abb`
  - `\cons`
- Added sample entries for the *Terms* index (6ca2670b)
